Helpful Tips!

  • Stores are always closed Sunday, so get all of your shopping done on Saturday! Restaurants and souvenir shops are open.
  • Save all glass jars and plastic bottles and return them to the grocery store for money back...it adds up!
  • Learn the public transporation system! The Stra√üenbahn (tram) is a great way to get around and you don't have to worry about parking.
  • There are apps that you can download to map out your route.
  • Dates are in day/month/year format instead of month/day/year! Become familiar with 24-hour clock, it's used everywhere in Heidelberg and Europe.
  • You bring your own cake on your birthday! Make sure to have one ready.
